How to apply limits?

From your Backoffice account open Sportsbook Management – Bet Limits – Sport Limits section. If you want to change Sport Limits, press the “Apply” button, and then edit the button from the top right corner. Insert limit percentage and press save.

Inserted value is a percentage from Selection Liability.

![](/static/media/how-to-apply-limits.216c1d19a7c3bf4a507f.png)

**How to change Competition and Market Type limits?**

If you want to change the limits of specific competition or market Type, from the Filter section choose Sport, Region, then press “Apply”. Repeating the same process as for Sport limits you can change the limits here as well.

**Note:**  Limit management is only available to users with permission.
