## 文档正文
Match Winner

Team to win more games out of Best of # series is the match up winner.

Game Betting

The first team to destroy the enemy Patron is declared the winner regardless of the current Frag score.

Duration

Over or under a certain time for a map to be completed. A duration the same as the threshold is resolved as OVER Selection. The duration is resolved based on the final score screen, Will be settled based on the official scoreboard, broadcast or game API.

Total Kills

Over or under a certain total kill count for a map. whether the final kill count in a Map (based on the visible in-game score, which is the sum of the kills by both teams) is over or under a certain figure.

Kill Handicap

Which team has more kills after considering a handicap.

Total Towers

Over or under a certain total destroyed towers for a map. whether the destroyed towers’ count in a Map (based on the visible in-game score, which is the sum of the destroyed towers by both teams) is over or under a certain figure.

Who will destroy the first Guardian

Are settled based on the first Guardian destroyed.
For settlement purposes all buildings destroyed count as being destroyed by the opposing team, regardless of whether the last hit was from a Hero or a Creep.

Who will destroy the first Walker

Are settled based on the first Walker destroyed.
For settlement purposes all buildings destroyed count as being destroyed by the opposing team, regardless of whether the last hit was from a Hero or a Creep.

Who will destroy the first Shrine

Are settled based on the first Shrine destroyed.
For settlement purposes all buildings destroyed count as being destroyed by the opposing team, regardless of whether the last hit was from a Hero or a Creep.

First Mid Boss Kill

The winner will be the team that kills the Mid Boss first.

Rejuvenator

The Rejuvenator is a large, green crystal that spawns after the Mid Boss is slain. After initially spawning it floats down. Once it touches the ground it can be picked up by smashing it with a Heavy Melee attack. Any player can do this regardless of which team has killed the Mid-Boss. Meaning that it can also be stolen. Once acquired the team gains the Rejuvenation Credit buff.

Both teams will kill the Mid Boss

Options "Yes" and "No". Option "Yes" will win if both teams kill the Mid Boss at least once in said game.

Kill markets

Frags are settled based on the number of heroes killed. Creep kills and hero kills by creeps do not count towards the current match score.
