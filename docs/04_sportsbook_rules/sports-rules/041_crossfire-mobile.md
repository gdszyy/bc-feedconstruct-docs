Map Betting

Victory is achieved by winning at least 10 rounds (or in accordance with the tournament regulations). In a situation with a draw on the map, when the rounds are 9-9, the Victory in overtime is awarded to the team that achieves an advantage with a gap of two clear rounds (11-9 12-10) in case of continuation of overtime until (12-12). Victory in overtime is awarded to the team that reaches the first score of 13.
