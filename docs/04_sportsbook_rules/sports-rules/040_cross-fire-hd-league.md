Map Betting

Victory is achieved by winning at least 9 rounds (or in accordance with the tournament regulations). In a situation with a draw on the map, when rounds are 8-8, Victory in overtime is awarded to the team that has achieved a lead of two clear rounds (10-8 10-9) in the event of a continuation overtime until (10-10). Victory in overtime is awarded to the first team to score 11 points.
