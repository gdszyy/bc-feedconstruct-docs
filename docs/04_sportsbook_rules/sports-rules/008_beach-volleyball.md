## 文档正文
Outright Betting is all-in compete or not. Where applicable the podium presentation will determine the settlement of bets. Subsequent disqualifications and/or appeals will not affect bets.

If the venue of the match (of the sport ground) changes, all bets stand.

If one of the announced participants in the pair is replaced before the start of the match, all bets on this match are refunded.

In the event of a match starting but not being completed then all bets will be void, unless the specific market outcome is already determined. Bets on markets not determined, even where a natural conclusion would cause an indisputable result, are void.

For example, a set is abandoned at 18-17: bets on Over/Under 35.5 Set - Total Points are void.

Current/Next Set Leader After: If the quoted number of points is not reached in the specified set, then the team who wins the set will be settled as the winner.
