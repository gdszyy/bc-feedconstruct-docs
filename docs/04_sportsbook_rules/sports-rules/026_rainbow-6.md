## 文档正文
Victory is achieved by winning at least 7 rounds (or in accordance with the tournament regulations). In a situation with a draw on the map, when the rounds are 6-6, the organizers can appoint additional rounds, so-called `overtime` (OT). The victory in overtime is awarded to the team that achieves an advantage with a gap of two clear rounds (8-6 9-7 10-8 11-9 etc.).

Match Up Winner

Winner will be a team who wins more Maps in Best of # series.

Race To Rounds

A bet on which team is the first to reach the chosen number of rounds on the specified map.
