BetResulted

The BetResulted method is called by FeedCosntruct when an accepted bet is settled, updated, or its result is canceled (i.e., the bet status or winning amount changes).

Important: The Partner may receive multiple BetResulted calls for the same bet.

Use Cases

Examples of when BetResulted may be triggered:

  • Returned Bet: The bet was rejected or voided. → BetState: 2, Amount: BetStakeAmount.
  • Won Bet: The bet won, and the calculated winnings are provided. → BetState: 4, Amount: Winning amount.
  • Lost Bet: The bet lost. → BetState: 3, Amount: 0.
  • Cashed-Out Bet: The bet was cashed out. → BetState: 5, Amount: Cashed-out amount (sent by the partner in the MarkBetAsCashout or CheckAndMarkBetAsCashout requests).
  • Reverted Result:The previously settled result was canceled and the bet was returned to its initial (unsettled) state. → BetState: 1, Amount: 0.

Important Notes:

  • Always use the Amount field to determine the net winning or refund for the player.
  • Do not link balance logic to BetState values. BetState is for informational purposes only.
  • If multiple BetResulted calls are received for the same bet:
    • Adjust the Client’s balance by the difference between the new Amount and the previously credited amount.

URL: /BetResulted

Example: http://partnerhostname:port/api/BetResulted

Where http://partnerhostname:port/api is the Partner’s Base URL

Method: POST
