GetMaxBetAmount

The GetMaxBetAmount endpoint returns the maximum allowable stake for a specific selection or bet combination. This call helps Partners determine the betting limits before placing a bet, ensuring that submitted stakes do not exceed the platform’s predefined thresholds.

Same as in case of CreateBet, if the mandatory fields of ClientDetailModel are not sent, FC calls GetClientDetails to identify the client and validate applicable stake limits. After getting the response, FC does

  • Client identification.
  • Risk assessment.
  • Max stake calculation and response delivery.

The processes described in 4, 5, 6, 10 points of the Process Flow - Bet Placement via Betguard API are actual here
