BetPlaced

When a bet passes all validations, FeedConstruct initiates the BetPlaced call to the Partner to request final confirmation.

During this stage, the bet remains in a pending state until a response is received from the Partner.

  • The Partner has 5 seconds to respond.
  • If no response is received within this timeframe, FeedConstruct automatically initiates a rollback, and the bet is not processed.

This mechanism ensures that only confirmed bets are accepted and helps prevent unresolved or stuck bet states due to communication delays.

URL: /BetPlaced

Example: http://partnerhostname:port/api/BetPlaced

Where http://partnerhostname:port/api is the Partner’s Base URL

Method: POST
