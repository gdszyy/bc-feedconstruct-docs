All bets are calculated based on 36 minutes game time, 3 periods of 12 minutes each unless stated otherwise.

Added time and penalty shootout are taken into account only for bets without draw offered in regulation game time, team to progress, tournament winner etc

The match will stand if play has been at least 24 minutes. All bets are calculated on the basis of the results at this point.

Match Winner

Where offered, bets are returned if a draw unless there is extra time (or another way to determine victory) when bets stand on official result.
